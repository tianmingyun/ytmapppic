# 切换期的选择：如果明天只剩三个人的联系方式，你会留谁？— 浓彩水粉画

## Scene Illustration — Letting Go of the Old System

FOCAL POINT: A solitary figure standing at a fork in a path, facing three warm glowing figures (core people) on a distant bright horizon while dozens of fading gray silhouettes (passersby / 路人) dissolve into mist behind them. The figure's hand is gently releasing a thin thread connected to the fading silhouettes — letting them go.

On the ground, scattered fragments of a broken "old system" — cracked gears, faded schedules, broken clocks — represent the old way of operating that no longer works.

ATMOSPHERE: Painterly gouache with thick visible brushwork. The foreground (old system debris, fading figures) is painted in cooler, muted tones. The distant horizon (core people) glows with warm golden light.

MOOD: Bittersweet but hopeful — the sadness of letting go mixed with the clarity of knowing what truly matters.
