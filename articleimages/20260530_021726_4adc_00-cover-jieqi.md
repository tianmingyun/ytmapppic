# 最近总感觉被推着走？你可能正处在一个关键切换期

## Atmospheric Cover — 浓彩水粉画

FOCAL POINT: A solitary figure standing at a crossroads between four distinct seasonal landscapes — a misty dormant winter field (蛰伏), a foggy confusion zone (迷茫), a sunlit spring meadow ascending upward (上升), and a swirling autumn vortex (躁动). The four zones blend into each other like a Chinese ink-wash meets gouache painting.

ATMOSPHERE: Soft diffused light, painterly brushwork with visible thick pigment strokes (浓彩水粉), warm-cool color contrast across the four zones. Dreamlike and contemplative.

MOOD: Reflective, hopeful, slightly melancholic — the feeling of being in transition between life seasons.

COLOR TEMPERATURE: Warm overall with cool undertones — rich gouache pigments.

STYLE: Thick watercolor/gouache painting style (浓彩水粉画). Visible brushstrokes, rich pigment layering, slight texture of handmade paper. Simplified human silhouette, not photorealistic. No realistic faces.
